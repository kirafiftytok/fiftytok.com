                        TEMPLATE 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY BRIEF
================================================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY BRIEF
========================

TOPIC: [Your research topic]
DATE: [Date]
AUTHOR: [Your name]
PREPARED FOR: [Audience/client]

---

What decision-makers need to know in 30 seconds:
[2-3 sentences. Lead with the insight, not the background.]

---

KEY FINDINGS

1. [Most important finding]
   → Implication: [What this means]

2. [Second finding]
   → Implication: [What this means]

3. [Third finding]
   → Implication: [What this means]

---

[Clear, specific recommendation. One sentence if possible.]

[Why this matters right now. Urgency driver.]

---

• [Stat or data point 1]
• [Stat or data point 2]
• [Stat or data point 3]

---

NEXT STEPS
□ [Action item 1] — Owner: [Name] — Due: [Date]

□ [Action item 3] — Owner: [Name] — Due: [Date]

[Link to full research document]
[Link to data sources]

================================================================================
                     BONUS: AI-ASSISTED RESEARCH PROMPTS
================================================================================

Use these prompts with ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI assistants to speed up
your research process:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT 1: FOR EXECUTIVE SUMMARIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'm writing an executive summary brief on [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. 
Here's my raw research: [PASTE NOTES]

Help me:
1. Identify the 3 most important findings
2. Write a "bottom line" statement (30 seconds to read)
3. Suggest one clear recommended action

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT 2: FOR COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I need to analyze [MY COMPANY] vs. [COMPETITORS]. 

Based on publicly available information, help me:
1. Create a comparison table for: pricing, features, market position
2. Identify 3 areas where we have advantages
3. Identify 3 areas where competitors beat us
4. Suggest 2-3 opportunity gaps we could exploit

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT 3: FOR MARKET RESEARCH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'm researching the [INDUSTRY] market. Help me structure a brief covering:
1. Market size and growth rate
2. Top 3 market drivers and headwinds
3. Key customer segments
4. 3 trends to watch (short, medium, long-term)

Focus on [GEOGRAPHIC REGION] and [TIME PERIOD].

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT 4: FOR TREND ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What are the top 5 trends in [INDUSTRY] right now?

For each trend, tell me:
1. Is it emerging, accelerating, maturing, or declining?
2. Impact level (high/medium/low)
3. What companies should do about it


================================================================================
                              HOW TO USE THIS PACK
================================================================================

1. PICK THE RIGHT TEMPLATE for your research type
2. FILL IN THE BRACKETS with your specific information
3. DELETE SECTIONS that don't apply to your situation
4. ADD SECTIONS if you need more detail for your audience
5. KEEP IT SCANNABLE — busy people read in bullets
6. LEAD WITH INSIGHTS — don't bury the good stuff at the end

PRO TIP: Use the AI prompts first to gather and organize your research,
THEN fill in the template. Much faster than starting from scratch.
