==========================================
     LANDING PAGE COPY TEMPLATES
==========================================

High-converting copy frameworks for sales pages, opt-in pages, and product launches.


------------------------------------------
PART 1: HEADLINE FORMULAS (25 FORMULAS)
------------------------------------------

THE PROMISE HEADLINE:
Get [Desired Result] in [Timeframe] Without [Objection]
Example: "Get Your First Client in 30 Days Without Cold Calling"

THE HOW-TO:
How to [Achieve Result] Even If [Objection]
Example: "How to Write Sales Copy Even If You Failed English Class"

THE NUMBER:
[Number] [Things] That [Promise]
Example: "7 Pricing Mistakes That Cost Freelancers $10K+ Per Year"

THE QUESTION:
Are You [Doing Thing Wrong]?
Example: "Are You Leaving Money on the Table with Every Proposal?"

THE "WHAT IF":
What If You Could [Outcome] Starting [Timeframe]?
Example: "What If You Could Double Your Email List Starting Next Week?"

THE SECRET:
The [Adjective] Secret [Audience] Uses to [Result]
Example: "The Simple Secret 6-Figure Freelancers Use to Charge More"

THE MISTAKE:
The #1 Mistake [Audience] Makes When [Activity]
Example: "The #1 Mistake Course Creators Make When Launching"

THE FORMULA:
The [Adjective] Formula for [Result]
Example: "The 3-Step Formula for Landing Pages That Convert"

THE WHO ELSE:
Who Else Wants [Desirable Outcome]?
Example: "Who Else Wants Clients Who Pay on Time?"

THE REASON WHY:
[Number] Reasons Why [Claim]
Example: "5 Reasons Why Your Homepage Isn't Converting"

THE NEGATIVE APPROACH:
Stop [Bad Thing] — Start [Good Thing] Instead
Example: "Stop Guessing Your Prices — Start Charging What You're Worth"

THE COMPARISON:
Why [Your Solution] Beats [Alternative] for [Audience]
Example: "Why Templates Beat Blank Pages for Busy Founders"

THE TESTIMONIAL LEAD:
"[Specific Result Quote]" — Here's How [Name] Did It
Example: "'I closed my first $5K client.' — Here's How Sarah Did It"

THE CONTRARIAN:
[Common Belief] Is Wrong — Here's What Actually Works
Example: "You Don't Need More Traffic — Here's What You Actually Need"

THE SPECIFIC NUMBER:
How I [Result] in [Exact Timeframe] With [Constraint]
Example: "How I Made $2,347 in 14 Days With Zero Ad Spend"

THE BEFORE/AFTER:
From [Bad State] to [Good State] in [Timeframe]
Example: "From 0 Subscribers to 1,000 in 60 Days"

THE FEAR:
[Number] Signs That [Bad Outcome] Is Happening to You
Example: "3 Signs Your Business Is Stuck (And What to Do About It)"

THE ASPIRATION:
Finally, [Desired State] Is Possible
Example: "Finally, Landing Pages You're Proud to Share"

THE GUARANTEE:
[Result] Guaranteed or [Offer]
Example: "Double Your Open Rates or Your Money Back"

THE EXCLUSIVE:
[Thing] You Won't Find Anywhere Else
Example: "The Pricing Strategy 7-Figure Consultants Won't Tell You"

THE CHALLENGE:
[Timeframe] to [Result]
Example: "30 Days to a Full Client Calendar"

THE SHORTCUT:
Skip [Hard Part] — Go Straight to [Good Part]
Example: "Skip the Copywriting Course — Get the Templates Instead"

THE WARNING:
Warning: [Thing] May [Negative Consequence]
Example: "Warning: Your About Page May Be Killing Your Sales"

THE CURIOSITY GAP:
[Audience] Are Using This [Method] — Are You?
Example: "Top Coaches Are Using This Sales Page Framework — Are You?"

THE TRANSFORMATION:
Turn [Current State] Into [Desired State]
Example: "Turn Website Visitors Into Email Subscribers"

PART 7: OBJECTION HANDLERS
------------------------------------------

"I'm not a copywriter"
You don't have to be. These are fill-in-the-blank templates. If you can describe your product, you can use these.

"I don't have time"
These save you time. Instead of starting from scratch, pick a template and fill in the blanks. Most people finish a landing page in under an hour.

"I can just find free templates online"
You can. But you'll spend hours finding them, vetting them, and stitching them together. This is already organized, tested, and ready to use.

"What if it doesn't work for my business?"
30-day money back guarantee. If you don't see improvement, email me and I'll refund you.

"I already bought a copywriting course"
Courses teach theory. This is the application. You can use these templates alongside any framework you've learned.

"My niche is different"
These frameworks work across niches because they're based on psychology, not industry specifics. You add the details; the structure stays the same.

PART 9: FINAL POLISH CHECKLIST
------------------------------------------

BEFORE YOU PUBLISH:

□ Headline makes a specific promise
□ Subheadline expands or qualifies headline
□ First paragraph hooks them or calls out their problem
□ Benefits are about THEM, not your product
□ CTA is clear and appears multiple times
□ Social proof is specific (numbers, names, results)
□ Objections are addressed before they become reasons to leave
□ Guarantee reduces risk
□ Formatting makes it scannable (bullets, headers, bold)
□ You've read it out loud (if it sounds weird, fix it)
