====================================================
        STORYTELLING FRAMEWORKS
====================================================


THE AIDA FRAMEWORK
-------------------

Attention: [Hook that stops the scroll]
Interest: [Why they should care]
Desire: [What they'll get/become]
Action: [What to do next]


THE BEFORE-AFTER-BRIDGE
------------------------

Before: [The painful/undesirable state]
After: [The ideal state they want]
Bridge: [How you/your product gets them there]


THE PAS FORMULA
----------------

Problem: [The pain point they're experiencing]
Agitate: [Make it worse—show the real cost]
Solve: [Present the solution]


THE 3-ACT STORY STRUCTURE
--------------------------

Act 1: The Setup (context, the "before")
Act 2: The Conflict (what happened, the struggle)
Act 3: The Resolution (the lesson, the transformation)


====================================================
        PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS
====================================================


INSTAGRAM
----------
• First line is everything (cut off after ~125 chars)
• Use line breaks for readability
• 3-5 relevant hashtags, not 30
• Emojis as visual breaks, not decoration
• End with a question or CTA


LINKEDIN
---------
• Hook in first 2 lines (before "see more")
• Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
• One idea per line works well
• No hashtags in body text (bottom only)
• Authenticity beats polish


TWITTER/X
----------
• Front-load the value
• Threads: make each tweet standalone valuable
• Use numbers and lists
• Quote tweet yourself for engagement
• Threads outperform single tweets for depth


TIKTOK
-------
• Caption is secondary to hook audio
• 3-5 hashtags max
• Match trending sounds when relevant
• #fyp still works but isn't magic
• Reply to comments with videos
