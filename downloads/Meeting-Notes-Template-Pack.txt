Thank you for your purchase! Here are 10 professional templates
to make your meetings more productive.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Copy the template you need
2. Paste into your preferred note-taking app
3. Fill in the brackets [like this] with your info
4. Delete any sections you don't need
5. Share with attendees after the meeting

Pro tip: Create a "meeting templates" folder in your notes app
and save each template for quick access.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
BONUS: MEETING NOTES CHEAT SHEET
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BEFORE THE MEETING:
□ Define the purpose in one sentence
□ Send agenda 24 hours ahead
□ Assign a note-taker

DURING THE MEETING:
□ Capture WHO said WHAT (not just topics)
□ Note decisions with exact wording
□ Record action items with OWNER and DUE DATE

AFTER THE MEETING:
□ Send notes within 24 hours
□ Highlight action items at the top
□ File notes where the team can find them

UNIVERSAL FORMULA:
MEETING NOTES = Context + Discussion + Decisions + Actions + Next Steps
