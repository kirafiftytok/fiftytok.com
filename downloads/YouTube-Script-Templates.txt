PART 1: VIDEO SCRIPT TEMPLATES

--------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 1: TUTORIAL / HOW-TO VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK (0-10 seconds):
"By the end of this video, you'll know exactly how to [specific outcome] — even if you've never done it before."

INTRO (10-30 seconds):
"[Problem they have]. I've been there. But after [experience/testing], I found a way that actually works. Let me show you step by step."

STEP 1:
"First, [action]. The reason this matters is [why]. Here's exactly what that looks like..."
[Show/demonstrate]

STEP 2:
"Next, [action]. A lot of people skip this, but it's crucial because [reason]."
[Show/demonstrate]

STEP 3:
"Now for [action]. This is where most tutorials lose you — but stay with me, it's simpler than it looks."
[Show/demonstrate]

BONUS/PRO TIP:
"One thing I wish someone told me earlier: [insight]. This saves you [time/money/frustration]."

RECAP:
"Quick recap: Step 1 was [X]. Step 2 was [Y]. Step 3 was [Z]. That's it — you now know how to [outcome]."

CTA:
"If this helped, hit like so I know to make more of these. Got questions? Drop them in the comments. And if you want [related content], check out [video]. See you there."


--------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 2: LISTICLE / TOP X VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"[Number] [things] that will change the way you [topic]. Number [X] honestly shocked me."

INTRO:
"I've spent [time] testing/researching/using these, and I'm giving you the unfiltered breakdown. No fluff, just what actually works. Let's go."

ITEM 1:
"Number one: [Item]. Why does this make the list? [Specific reason]. I've personally used this for [use case], and [result]. Moving on..."

ITEM 2:
"Number two: [Item]. This one's underrated. Most people overlook it because [reason], but here's why that's a mistake..."

[Continue pattern for each item: Name → Why it matters → Personal experience/proof → Transition]

ITEM X (Best/Most Surprising):
"And finally, number [X]: [Item]. I saved this for last because [reason]. This one completely changed [specific thing] for me."

WRAP-UP:
"So there you have it — [number] [things] that actually [deliver on promise]. My personal favorite? Probably [pick one and briefly say why]."

CTA:
"Which one are you trying first? Let me know in the comments. And if you found value here, subscribe — I drop [content type] every [frequency]. See you in the next one."


--------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 3: STORY / PERSONAL EXPERIENCE
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"[Dramatic statement or result]. Let me tell you how it happened."

SETUP (The Before):
"[Time period] ago, I was [situation]. I didn't know [what you know now]. And honestly? I was [emotional state/problem]."

THE TURNING POINT:
"Then [event happened]. At first, I didn't think much of it. But looking back, this was the moment everything changed."

THE JOURNEY:
"I started [action]. It wasn't easy — [specific challenge]. But I kept going because [motivation]. Then [what happened next]."

THE BREAKTHROUGH:
"And then [the moment of success]. I remember [specific detail]. It was the first time I realized [insight]."

THE LESSON:
"Here's what I learned: [The takeaway]. If you're [in their situation], know that [encouragement + practical advice]."

CTA:
"Have you experienced something similar? I'd love to hear your story in the comments. And if you want to see [related content], I made a whole video on that — link's [in description/above]. Talk soon."


--------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 4: PRODUCT REVIEW / COMPARISON
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"Is [product] worth it? I bought it, used it for [timeframe], and here's my honest take — including the stuff no one talks about."

CONTEXT:
"First, who is this for? If you're [ideal user profile], keep watching. If you're [not ideal user], this probably isn't for you. Cool? Let's get into it."

WHAT IT IS:
"Quick overview: [Product] is [brief description]. It costs [price] and claims to [main promise]. Let's see if it delivers."

WHAT I LIKED:
"The good stuff. First, [pro 1] — here's why that matters... Second, [pro 2]. Third, [pro 3]. These genuinely impressed me."

WHAT I DIDN'T LIKE:
"Now the not-so-good. [Con 1] — this was frustrating because [reason]. [Con 2] — not a dealbreaker, but worth knowing. [Con 3] — [honest take]."

THE VERDICT:
"So, should you buy it? Here's my honest answer: If you're [specific use case], yes — it's worth it. If you're [different use case], maybe look at [alternative] instead."

CTA:
"Have you tried [product]? What was your experience? Drop it in the comments. And if you want my full breakdown on [related topic], check out [video]. See you there."


--------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 5: REACTION / COMMENTARY VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"I just saw [thing] and I have THOUGHTS. Let's break this down."

SETUP:
"So here's what happened: [Brief context]. If you haven't seen it, here's the clip / here's what they said..."

REACTION:
"Okay, first reaction: [Initial thoughts]. But let me actually think about this..."

ANALYSIS:
"Here's what I think is really going on: [Deeper take]. The reason this matters is [why]. And here's what most people are missing..."

MULTIPLE ANGLES:
"On one hand, [perspective 1]. But on the other hand, [perspective 2]. Personally? I lean toward [your take] because [reason]."

CONCLUSION:
"Look, at the end of the day, [final thought]. [Prediction or call to awareness]."

CTA:
"What do you think? Am I off base? Tell me in the comments — I actually read them. And if you want more [content type], hit subscribe. See you next time."


--------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 6: EDUCATIONAL / EXPLAINER VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"[Topic] explained in [time]. No jargon. No confusion. Just the stuff that actually matters."

WHY CARE:
"Why should you care about [topic]? Because [concrete reason it affects them]. Most people don't understand this, and it costs them [time/money/opportunity]."

THE BASICS:
"Let's start simple. [Topic] is basically [simple definition]. Think of it like [analogy]. Make sense so far?"

GOING DEEPER:
"Now here's where it gets interesting. [Layer 2 of complexity]. This matters because [connection to their life/work]."

THE KEY INSIGHT:
"The thing most people miss: [Crucial insight]. Once you understand this, everything else clicks."

PRACTICAL APPLICATION:
"So how do you actually use this? Here's a real example: [Concrete scenario]. See how that works?"

SUMMARY:
"Quick recap: [Topic] is [1-sentence definition]. The key insight is [main point]. And you can apply it by [action]."

CTA:
"Want to go deeper on this? I made a whole [video/playlist] on [related topic]. Link's [location]. And if this clicked for you, like and subscribe — more [topic] content coming."

HOOK:

"Now I'm [activity]. What you're seeing here is [explanation]. The reason I [do it this way] is [insight]."

"Quick moment — [honest reflection or behind-the-scenes thought]. Not everything's perfect, but that's real life."

"Moving on to [next thing]. This is the part I [love/dread] because [reason]. Let me show you..."

"Okay, [something went wrong or unexpected happened]. Here's how I handled it..."

WRAP-UP:

TEMPLATE 8: Q&A / FAQ VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"I get asked these questions all the time. Today I'm answering [number] of them — including the one I usually avoid."

SETUP:
"These questions came from [source — comments, DMs, Patreon, etc.]. Let's just dive in."

QUESTION 1:
"First question, from [name/anonymous]: '[Question]' — Great question. The short answer is [direct answer]. The longer answer is [explanation]..."

QUESTION 2:
"Next up: '[Question]' — This one's interesting because [context]. My take is [answer]..."

[Continue pattern — each question gets: attribution → direct answer → elaboration]

THE AVOIDED QUESTION:
"And finally, the one I usually dodge: '[Hard question]'. Honestly? [Real answer]. I don't talk about this much because [reason], but [genuine response]."

CTA:
"Got more questions? Drop them in the comments — I might answer in a future video. And if you're new here, subscribe for [what you make]. Thanks for watching."

TEMPLATE 9: MYTH-BUSTING / DEBUNKING VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"Everything you've heard about [topic] is wrong. Well, most of it. Let me explain."

MYTH 1:
"Myth number one: '[Common belief].' Why people think this: [reason]. The truth? [Reality + evidence]. This matters because [practical implication]."

MYTH 2:
"Myth two: '[Belief].' I believed this for [time] — until I [learned/experienced otherwise]. Here's what's actually going on..."

WHAT TO DO INSTEAD:
"So if [topic] doesn't work that way, what should you actually do? [Practical alternative]. This is what [experts/successful people] actually do."

CTA:
"Were any of these surprising? Tell me in the comments which myth you believed. And if you want the full guide to [topic], I made a video on that — link's [location]."

TEMPLATE 10: CHALLENGE / TRANSFORMATION VIDEO
--------------------------------------------

HOOK:
"I tried [challenge] for [time period]. Here's what happened."

THE RULES:
"Here's what I committed to: [Rule 1], [Rule 2], [Rule 3]. No exceptions. Starting [date]."

WEEK/DAY 1:
"Day one: [Initial state/feelings]. This is harder/easier than I expected because [reason]. First observations..."

"[Time marker]: Starting to notice [change]. The hard part was [challenge]. But I pushed through because [motivation]."

THE LOW POINT:
"Around [time], I hit a wall. [What happened]. I almost [quit/gave up]. But here's what kept me going..."

THE END:
"Final day. [Final state]. Comparing to day one: [specific before/after]."

RESULTS & VERDICT:
"So was it worth it? Here are the concrete results: [Measurable outcomes]. Would I recommend it? [Honest assessment for different people]."

CTA:
"Want me to try [another challenge]? Comment what you'd want to see. And if you want to try this yourself, I put together [resource] — link in description. Good luck."

PART 4: BEST PRACTICES CHEAT SHEET
============================================

RETENTION TIPS
--------------
• Hook in first 5-10 seconds (critical for algorithm)
• Use pattern interrupts every 30-60 seconds
• Verbal markers: "Here's the thing," "But wait," "Now pay attention"
• Preview what's coming: "In a minute, I'll show you..."

SCRIPT WRITING RULES
--------------------
• Write like you talk (read it out loud)
• Short sentences = better delivery
• Leave room for personality and ad-libs
• Mark emphasis and pauses if it helps

FORMATTING NOTES
----------------
• Use headers for each section
• Bold or highlight key points
• Include visual cues: [SHOW SCREEN], [CUT TO B-ROLL]
• Keep approximate timestamps for pacing

THE GOLDEN RULE
---------------
Your script is a guide, not a prison. The best videos 
feel natural, not robotic. Use these templates as 
structure, then bring your personality.
