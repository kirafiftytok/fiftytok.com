PART 1: IDEA VALIDATION FRAMEWORK
------------------------------------------

Before you build anything, answer these 10 questions:

1. WHO IS THIS FOR?
   - Specific person (not "everyone")
   - What's their biggest frustration right now?
   - Where do they hang out online?

2. WHAT PROBLEM DOES IT SOLVE?
   - Can you describe it in one sentence?
   - Is this a "nice to have" or "need to have"?
   - How urgently do they need this solved?

3. WHY WOULD THEY PAY YOU?
   - What makes your solution different?
   - Why you instead of competitors?
   - What's your unfair advantage?

4. IS THERE MONEY HERE?
   - Are people already paying for similar solutions?
   - What are competitors charging?
   - Can you find 10 potential customers in 10 minutes?

5. CAN YOU REACH THEM?
   - Where can you find these people today?
   - Can you get in front of them for free?
   - What platform do they use most?

6. CAN YOU DELIVER?
   - Do you have the skills (or can you learn fast)?
   - How long would the first version take?
   - What's the minimum viable service?

7. PASSION CHECK
   - Would you still do this after 6 months?
   - Does this energize or drain you?
   - Can you talk about this topic for hours?

8. QUICK WIN TEST
   - Could you get your first customer this week?
   - What would a $100 version look like?
   - Who could you pitch today?

9. SCALE POTENTIAL
   - Can this grow beyond just you?
   - What would 10x revenue look like?
   - Is there a product you could create later?

10. EXIT OPTIONS
    - Could you sell this business someday?
    - Is it dependent on you specifically?
    - What would make someone want to buy it?

VALIDATION SCORE:
Give each answer a score from 1-10.
- 70+: Green light, start building
- 50-70: Promising, refine the idea
- Below 50: Keep brainstorming

PART 3: PRICING CALCULATOR
------------------------------------------

COST-BASED PRICING:
Your hourly rate goal: $______
Hours per project: ______
Direct costs per project: $______
Profit margin target: ______%

Minimum price = (Hours × Rate + Costs) × (1 + Margin)
= ____________________________

VALUE-BASED PRICING:
What's this worth to the customer? $______
What do competitors charge? $______
What would make this a no-brainer? $______

Sweet spot price: $______

PRICING RULES:
✓ Always include 3 tiers (good/better/best)
✓ Never compete on price alone
✓ Raise prices after 5 customers at each tier
✓ Test pricing—you can always adjust

PART 12: SIDE HUSTLE LAUNCH CHECKLIST
------------------------------------------

BEFORE YOU START:
□ Idea validated (10+ people said they'd pay)
□ One-page business plan complete
□ Pricing set (3 tiers)
□ Simple contract ready
□ Invoice template ready

WEEK 1:
□ Tell 10 friends/family what you're doing
□ Post on social media announcing your service
□ Reach out to 5 potential clients
□ Set up simple payment method (PayPal, Stripe, Venmo)
□ Create one piece of content showing your expertise

WEEK 2:
□ Follow up with all Week 1 contacts
□ Reach out to 10 more potential clients
□ Post 3x on your main platform
□ Ask 3 people for referrals

WEEK 3:
□ Close your first client (or find out why not)
□ Start collecting testimonials
□ Refine your pitch based on feedback
□ Reach out to 10 more people

WEEK 4:
□ Review what's working/not working
□ Adjust pricing if needed
□ Set up recurring marketing habit
□ Plan next month's goals
